How To Apply For Polytechnic Diploma Engineering Online Admission In Park Polytechnic Institute

Now everybody knows how to apply for online admission. Online Admission is the easiest process for student admission. During the corona-virus pandemic, all the educational institutions are locked down. But to continue the admission process for diploma in engineering program under BTEB, Park polytechnic institute introduced an online admission process for SSC passed students 2020 session.

9 simple steps to complete polytechnic online admission for diploma in the engineering program at park polytechnic.

  • 1. Go to google and search DPI Website then visit (ppi.com)
  • 2. Click on the Online Admission button on the right at the home page.
  • 3. Admission form will open
  • 4. Fill up the form with the required information
  • 5. Attach your photo, SSC mark sheet, and parent’s NID.
  • 6. Read the acknowledgment and tick to and then click submit button
  • 7. Then you will get confirmation of successful submission and click yes to proceed for make payment (If you click on “No” you will get a notification to pay within 24 hours otherwise your form will be rejected)
  • 8. If you click on “Yes” then choose your desired payment option and confirm your payment.
  • 9. A successful admission email will be sent to your email.
